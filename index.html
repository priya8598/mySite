Native

It is part of Kongu Nadu & Mazhanadu, a vast region that dates to the 2nd century BCE. The name 'Salem' appears to have been derived from the word 'Sela' or 'Shalya' which refers to 'the country around the hills'. Local tradition claims Salem as the birth place of the Tamil poetess Avvaiyar.
Salem was the largest district of Tamil Nadu. It was bifurcated into Salem – Dharmapuri districts in 1965 and Namakkal district in 1997. Now Salem has been developed a lot by building many bridges and is considered to be the smart city.
Salem Steel Plant, a special steels unit of Steel Authority of India Ltd, pioneered the supply of wider width stainless steel sheets / coils in India.
Salem Mango belt contributes the economy in large scale by exporting mangoes to foreign countries and supplying mangoes all over India.

Silence

is the thing I love about myself

"When used with intention and purpose, silence is a communication superpower.” Silence can be an obvious answer. Sometimes when we communicate we say too much. We over-explain. If a question is met with silence, there is often an answer in that silence. We can also soften the blow of a negative answer by silence being the response. There is an implied “no” without any harsh words or too many words that might do more harm than good. Another thing when someone says something we don’t agree with or find offensive. If we are silent, we send a powerful message that communicates that we don’t agree or are not going along with what someone is saying. Often our nonverbal language is a more powerful way of communicating than our verbal language.
